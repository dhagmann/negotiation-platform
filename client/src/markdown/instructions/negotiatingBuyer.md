## Negotiation Instructions

After you are paired with a participant and the negotiation begins, you will see a screen like this:

![](/assets/images/buyer-screenshot.png)

At the top of the screen, you will see a **timer** that shows how much time you have remaining in your negotiation. You will have **7 minutes** to negotiate, and the time will start counting down when you and your counterpart are both in the chat. If you cannot reach an agreement when the time is up, for example because you think the third party offers you a better deal than what your counterpart is offering you, the negotiation will end and you will receive the bonus associated with this (unknown) third party's offer.

On the left side, you will be able to access all the information about the negotiation once again. You can refer to this throughout the negotiation.

On the right side, you see a text window where you can chat with your counterpart. Remember that this is a real participant who, like you, is joining from Prolific. So please be considerate in your interaction, even if you have a difficult negotiation.

Above the chat window is a button for you to make an offer to your counterpart. They will also be able to make offers to you. Once either of you has made an offer, the other negotiator will get a popup box asking them if they want to accept or reject the offer. You can only make one offer every 30 seconds to avoid spam.
