## Negotiating Tips 

- **Ask Questions**: You can always try to learn more about what the other side wants---though they may or may not share everything, and they may or may not always tell you the truth.

- **Explain Your Position**: Make clear why you need a certain price or why the other person's offer isn't good enough for you.
