## Your Goal

- **If you are the Buyer**: Try to pay as little as possible.
- **If you are the Seller**: Try to sell at the highest price you can.

**But there is a twist.** Each side has an alternative deal if this negotiation does not lead to an agreement: a third party that will sell a battery unit to GreenBuy Energy and another party that will buy the existing one from SellTech Co. They will step in if you and the other participant cannot reach an agreement, and your bonus earnings will be based on what they offer. The offer from these third parties may or may not be better than what the other participant is willing or able to offer to you. So one challenge in this simulation will be to determine **whether to agree to a deal at all**, or whether you are better off walking away.

On the next screens, you will learn what your role will be (Buyer or Seller). You will also receive more information about the background of this negotiation and how your bonus earnings will be determined.
