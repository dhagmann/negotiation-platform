## What if you cannot reach an agreement with GreenBuy Energy?

You hired a consultant to do some market research for you. **Based on their experience, you could find another Buyer offering at least $3 million for your unit.** That said, the consultant also thinks that market conditions can change, and so there is some uncertainty.

But of course, SellTech Co wants to get as high a price as possible and even $5+ million doesn't seem impossible to achieve. And a low sale price would run the risk of setting a bad precedent for future deals.

Remember that you will earn a bonus of 10 cents for each $100k of the final sales price above a price of $1.5m. That final sales price is determined by the amount you agree on in the negotiation if you reach an agreement. If you do not reach an agreement, the price (and your bonus) will be determined by the true value of what another Buyer would offer your company. The price of the other option is probably not too far from the consultant's estimate, but it is not necessarily exactly that.
