## Role-Specific Information for SellTech Co

Your company, SellTech Co, has recently launched an advanced energy storage unit, which combines new technology that dramatically improves energy efficiency. This has attracted the interest of GreenBuy Energy as a potential buyer. SellTech Co is under pressure to recover significant R&D costs quickly, and the sale to GreenBuy Energy is seen as a critical first step in establishing market demand and setting a benchmark price.

SellTech Co believes that GreenBuy Energy's need for the storage unit is urgent, as they are struggling with outdated energy systems that limit their project capabilities. You suspect that GreenBuy Energy does not have viable alternative suppliers that can meet their technical requirements, giving you confidence that you hold a strong negotiating position.

If GreenBuy Energy delays their renewable energy project by six months, you estimate that they lose approximately $100,000 in revenue per month due to reduced efficiency. Therefore, if they fail to come to an agreement with you it really could be a bigger loss for them.

GreenBuy Energy, moreover, is not the only potential buyer. You have heard that some of their competitors are also interested in your new energy storage technology. So you think it is important to close this deal only if you can get a good price for the unit.
