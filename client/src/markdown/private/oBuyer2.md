## What if you cannot reach an agreement with SellTech Co? 

You hired a consultant to do some market research for you. **Based on their experience, you could find another Seller offering an equivalent unit at a price of at most $2.5 million.** That said, the consultant also thinks that market conditions can change, and so there is some uncertainty.

GreenBuy Energy's objective is to pay as little as possible for the unit. This shouldn't be hard since SellTech Co seems to be in such a weak position. Even buying for as low as $1 million doesn't seem impossible to achieve.

Remember that you will earn a bonus of 10 cents for each $100k of the final sales price below a price of $4.0m. That final sales price is determined by the amount you agree on in the negotiation if you reach an agreement. If you do not reach an agreement, the price (and your bonus) will be determined by the true value of what another Seller would charge your company. The price of the other option is probably not too far from the consultant's estimate, but it is not necessarily exactly that.
